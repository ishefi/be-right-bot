How to Download WhatsApp Chat

  • Go to the WhatsApp chat you want to botify.
  • Click on options
  • Click on More
  • Click on Export Chat
  • Choose Without Media
  • Email the chat to yourself
  • Download the chat and upload it here

How to Create a Telegram Bot and Retrieve Its Token

  • Talk to Botfather on Telegram
  • Use the /newbot command and name your bot.
  • Copy the token Botfather gave you and paste it here.
